About me

I am a PhD student in Applied Mathematics for the Life and Social Sciences at Arizona State University.

I received my Master in Science in Applied Mathematics for the Life and Social Sciences in 2021 sponsored by the Simon A. Levin Mathematical, Computational, and Modeling Sciences Center at Arizona State University.

I received my B. Sc. in Mathematics from the Yachay Tech University, where under the mentorship of Prof. Juan Mayorga-Zambrano, I worked on: Concentration and Multiplicity of Solutions for a Non-Linear Schrödinger Equation with critical Frequency: Exponential Case.
